Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 17 January 2026

O'Rekun Media ("Company", "we", "us", "our") operates the Divine Link application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Software.

Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our service. We do not sell your personal information. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for account authentication, subscription management, and important communications
  • Name: If provided during payment processing

1.2 Device Information

To manage your device limit and provide support, we collect:

  • Device identifier: A unique ID to identify your device
  • Device name and model: To help you identify registered devices
  • Operating system version: For compatibility and support purposes
  • App version: To ensure you have the latest updates

1.3 Subscription and Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full payment card details. We receive:

  • Subscription status (active, cancelled, etc.)
  • Billing period dates
  • Payment confirmation (not card numbers)

1.4 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the Software:

  • Feature usage patterns (aggregated and anonymised)
  • Error reports and crash logs
  • Performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Divine Link
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Authenticate your account and verify your subscription
  • Enforce device limits as per our Terms of Service
  • Send important updates about the Software or your account
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the UK or European Economic Area, we process your data based on:

Purpose Legal Basis
Providing the service Contract performance
Processing payments Contract performance
Device management Legitimate interest
Service communications Legitimate interest
Fraud prevention Legitimate interest
Legal compliance Legal obligation

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Service Providers: Third parties that help us operate our business (e.g., Stripe for payments, Supabase for authentication)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of O'Rekun Media, our users, or the public

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide you services
  • Required by law or for legitimate business purposes

After account deletion, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days for backup purposes and as required for legal compliance.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@orekunmedia.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the UK/EEA. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities.

9. Children's Privacy

Divine Link is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. Third-Party Services

Divine Link integrates with third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Stripe: Payment processing - Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Authentication and database - Privacy Policy

11. Advertising

11.1 Free Tier Advertisements

The free version of Divine Link (Mercy tier) displays advertisements within the application sidebar. These advertisements help support the continued development and maintenance of the free version.

11.2 Advertisement Data

Our advertising system may collect:

  • Ad impressions: Which advertisements are displayed
  • Ad interactions: Whether advertisements are clicked
  • Device information: General device type for ad targeting

We do not share personal identifying information with advertisers. Ad data is collected in aggregate form.

11.3 Opting Out of Advertisements

You can remove advertisements by upgrading to a paid subscription (Grace or Love tier). Paid subscribers do not see any advertisements within the application.

12. Cookies and Tracking

The Divine Link desktop application does not use cookies. Our website may use essential cookies for functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies on our website.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the Software. Your continued use after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
